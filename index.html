What is Loafathon???

Loafathon is a community running / hiking / walking event that takes place at Sugarloaf Park in Granville, Ohio. It is a six hour celebration of vert on the finest 92' of hill that Ohio has to offer. Come for one loaf, come for ten, or stay the full six hours — Loafathon is for everyone.
