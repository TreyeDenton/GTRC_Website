What is Loafathon???

Loafathon is a community running / hiking / walking event that takes place at Sugarloaf Park in Granville, Ohio. It is a six hour celebration of vert on the finest 92' hill that Ohio has to offer. Come for one loaf, come for ten, or stay the full six hours — Loafathon is for everyone.

FAQ

Q: But seriously, you guys just run up and down a hill for six hours?

A: Correct. It's an extremely simple event with an embarrassingly strong following.

Q: Why would anyone do that?

A: For fitness, for fun, for the view, for the group energy, or for a decent snack. Note: It's BYOS.

Q: Do I have to stay all six hours?

A: No. Stay for one loaf or many. Nobody is taking attendance.

Q: Do I have to run?

A: No. Walk it, hike it, shuffle it. Sugarloaf does not care.

Q: Is it competitive?

A: Only if you make it competitive.

Q: What if I've never done anything like this before?

A: Then this is an excellent time to start doing questionable things with a supportive group.
